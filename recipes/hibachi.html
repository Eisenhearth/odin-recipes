Hibachi Chicken

Description

This Hibachi Chicken is just like the kind from Benihana – it’s a delicious Japanese-inspired dish served with fried rice and veggies.

Ingredients

  1. Chicken breasts
  2. Garlic
  3. Sesame oil
  4. Soy sauce
  5. Hoisin sauce
  6. Ginger

Cooking Guide

  • Mix the hibachi chicken ingredients together.
  • Cook the chicken in a skillet
  • Serve with your Favorite Drink

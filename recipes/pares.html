Description

This Filipino braised beef stew is everyone’s comfort food! It’s so flavorful and the meat is cooked until fork-tender.
Make sure to serve this with fried garlic rice and soup!

Ingredients

  1. 2 lbs beef brisket (or shank), cut into cubes
  2. 5 cups water (for boiling)
  3. 4 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 1 medium onion, chopped
  5. 2-inch piece of ginger, sliced
  6. 3 tbsp soy sauce
  7. 3 tbsp oyster sauce
  8. 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  9. 2 tbsp brown sugar
  10. 1 tbsp fish sauce
  11. 2-3 pcs star anise
  12. 1 tsp five-spice powder (optional but recommended)
  13. ½ tsp black pepper
  14. 1 cup beef broth (or water)
  15. 2 tbsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water (for thickening)
  16. 2 tbsp cooking oil

Cooking Guide

Step 1

  • Add the beef to a pot and pour enough water to cover it completely.
  • Bring to a boil and let it simmer for 10 minutes to remove impurities.
  • Drain and rinse the beef with clean water. This step ensures a clear, flavorful broth.

Step 2

  • Heat 2 tbsp of cooking oil in a separate pot over medium heat.
  • Sauté the minced garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.
  • Add the boiled beef and stir-fry for a few minutes to allow it to absorb the aromatics.
  • Pour in the soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, and fish sauce. Stir to coat the beef evenly.
  • Add brown sugar, black pepper, star anise, and five-spice powder. Stir well.
  • Pour in the beef broth (or water) and bring the mixture to a boil. Lower the heat and let it simmer for 1.5 to 2 hours or until the beef is fork-tender.
  • Stir occasionally and add water if necessary.
  • Once the beef is tender, dissolve the cornstarch in water and pour it into the stew. Stir continuously until the sauce thickens.
  • Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. Remove from heat and set aside.
